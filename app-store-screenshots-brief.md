1. **SSH terminal** — Full PTY terminal emulation on iPhone/iPad
2. **Secure authentication** — Secure Enclave keys (biometric), imported keys, Keychain passwords
3. **Voice input** — On-device WhisperKit speech-to-text for hands-free terminal commands
4. **Connection management** — Save, organize, and quick-connect to SSH profiles
5. **Appearance customization** — 9 color themes, 4 monospace fonts, adjustable font size
6. **Host key verification** — TOFU + QR code fingerprint scanning
7. **Background reconnect** — Auto-reconnect and terminal state restore on foreground
8. **Keyboard hotkeys** — Quick access to Ctrl+C, Ctrl+Z, Ctrl+D, and other common shortcuts
