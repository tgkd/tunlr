tunlr

SSH terminal for iOS.

Features

Full terminal emulation with xterm-256color support. Save connection profiles and reconnect with a tap. Auto-reconnect restores your session when you switch back to the app.

Authenticate with Secure Enclave keys protected by Face ID, imported Ed25519 or ECDSA keys, or passwords stored in Keychain. Host key verification uses trust-on-first-use with QR code scanning for fingerprint import.

Voice Input

Dictate terminal commands using on-device speech recognition powered by WhisperKit. No audio leaves your device.

Customizable

9 color themes, 4 monospace fonts, adjustable font size, and a keyboard toolbar with shortcut packs for Shell, Tmux, Vim, and Claude.

Private by Design

No accounts, no analytics, no tracking. Everything runs on your device.
