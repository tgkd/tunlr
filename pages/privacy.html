# Privacy Policy

Effective 2026-04-12

This privacy policy applies to the tunlr app for iOS, created by Pavel Trofimov.

## Data Collection

The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data remains on your device.

SSH connection profiles, appearance preferences, and known host keys are stored locally. SSH private keys and passwords are stored in the iOS Keychain with biometric protection.

## Voice Input

Voice input uses on-device speech recognition (WhisperKit). Audio is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

## Network Access

The app connects only to SSH servers that you explicitly configure. It does not contact any other servers, analytics endpoints, or third-party services.

## Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children

The app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes

This policy may be updated. Changes will be posted on this page.
